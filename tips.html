Travel Tips for Taniti Island

Electrical & Currency

Power outlets are 120 volts (same as the U.S.). Taniti uses the U.S. dollar, though euros and yen are often accepted. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.

Alcohol & Drinking Age

Alcohol is not served or sold between midnight and 9:00 a.m. The drinking age is 18, but enforcement is not strict.

Language

Many younger Tanitians speak fluent English. Very little English is spoken in rural areas, especially by older residents.

Health & Safety

There is one hospital and several clinics. Violent crime is very rare, though pickpocketing and petty crimes may occur. The hospital has multilingual staff.

Holidays

Taniti enjoys many national holidays. Many tourist attractions and restaurants will be closed on these days, so plan accordingly.
